Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how ApeXBlock (the "Game") handles user information and personal data.

Direct Summary: ApeXBlock is built as an offline-first, client-side, fully standalone block puzzle game. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data or personal identifying information (PII). All gameplay records, scores, custom shapes, and skin purchase states are stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

ApeXBlock does not require any account creation, user registration, social media linking, or login credentials to play. As such, we do not collect or request information including, but not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, address, or contact details;
  • Financial transaction information or credit card numbers;
  • Your location, phone number, contacts list, or device files;
  • Any biometric identifiers or browsing history.

2. Local Data Storage & Standalone Nature

The Game employs standard sandbox local data persistence patterns (using Android Room SQLite database and SharedPreferences) to record:

  • Game progress, coin counts accumulated through free in-game achievements, and high score rankings;
  • Settings and game configurations (e.g. background music, sound effects volume, and 3D display mode settings);
  • Your custom designed block shapes generated inside the built-in Block Creator studio.

This data remains strictly contained inside the app's secure internal sandbox region on your physical Android device. It is never uploaded to external servers or accessible to third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

ApeXBlock is fully suitable for all age ranges and does not consciously collect information from children under the age of 13. Because our software does not communicate with external central backends, children's device integrity and identity remains entirely secured.

4. Advertising and Analytics

The Game does not currently integrate third-party ad networks, telemetry trackers, profiling tools, or analytics SDKs. Enjoy completely secure, uninterrupted gameplay with no profiling or target advertising.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any updates occur, they will be reflected directly on this page. We encourage players to periodically check this declaration to stay fully informed of our persistent zero-data-collection philosophy.

6. Contact Us

For any comments, queries, or assistance regarding our privacy protection paradigms, please reach out to our team at support@ape-x-block.com.
